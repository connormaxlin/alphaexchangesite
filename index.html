Buy Side Solutions

Our members strategically request bespoke research, data, models, and insights at the touch of a button, allowing them to save time and receive real time responses from the right professionals. With MiFID II increasing pressure on asset managers to scrutinise and justify their research spend, we help buyers comply with the upcoming regulatory burden by providing easily discoverable pools of quality research from a wider audience of specialists

Research Provider Solutions

We allow Independent Research Providers, Individual Specialists, and Sell Side Brokers to privately answer hundreds of requests for research, data, models, and projects. By connecting you with a wide universe of institutional clients representing over $1 trillion in assets under management, we help you penetrate asset manager firewalls and get discovered! You’ll be able to set your rate for calls, meetings, research reports, and bespoke projects, while we take care of all the billing and payments on one central platform, so you can focus on creating your professional profile and promoting your expertise.

Investor Relations Solutions

Get direct access to your shareholders by answering investors’ most pressing questions. Discover new investors who might be interested in owning your stock. Arrange calls and meetings directly on the platform with new and existing shareholders.
